# Description (4000 char max)

MirrorKit is the simplest way to display your iPhone screen on your Mac. Plug in your iPhone with a USB cable and your screen appears instantly — in a beautiful device frame, in real time, with zero latency.

Built natively for macOS using the same screen capture technology as QuickTime Player, MirrorKit is fast, lightweight, and respects your privacy. No accounts, no cloud, no subscriptions. Buy it once and own it forever.


KEY FEATURES

• Real-time mirroring over USB — no Wi-Fi, no AirPlay, no lag
• Authentic iPhone device frame with Dynamic Island for a polished look
• Fullscreen mode with a beautiful gradient background, perfect for presentations
• Always-on-top toggle to keep your iPhone visible while you work
• Floating toolbar that stays out of your way
• Menu bar status item for quick access
• Native macOS app — under 5 MB, instant launch, low CPU usage
• Works with every iPhone running iOS 13 or later


PERFECT FOR

• Demoing iOS apps to clients, colleagues, or your audience
• Recording app tutorials and screencasts in stunning quality
• Live streaming your iPhone gameplay or workflows
• Debugging and developing iOS apps without an external monitor
• Keeping your iPhone visible during long Mac sessions
• Showing photos and videos to a group on a larger screen


PRIVACY FIRST

MirrorKit runs entirely on your Mac. Your iPhone screen never leaves your computer. Nothing is uploaded, recorded, or transmitted. The app only requests Camera permission because macOS treats your iPhone as a video capture device — exactly like QuickTime Player does. We collect zero data, period.


REQUIREMENTS

• macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
• An iPhone running iOS 13 or later
• A USB or USB-C cable to connect your iPhone to your Mac
• You may need to "Trust" your Mac on the iPhone the first time you connect
